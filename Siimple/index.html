About us...

“Agricultural Land Management System” is to facilitate agricultural land for farmers those who want to do agriculture but they don’t have land. Through our site the sellers (land owners) those who want to give the land for lease and the buyer (farmers) those who want the land by lease for their agricultural purpose can communicate directly. The registration of the land in the site can be completey done only after getting the approval from the village officer of the particular areas. Land is generally rented either under fixed price rental agreement or under sharecropping agreement. It will help the farmers to easily approach the landlords for getting rented lands for cultivation. Those who wish to lease land can search the land details from anywhere at any time. We provide a customer care service for the farmers which will help them to get vehicles, machines, fertilizers and other materials for rent.
